Delicious Homemade Lasagna

This is a classic lasagna recipe that's perfect for family dinners!

Description

This classic homemade lasagna is rich, cheesy, and packed with hearty flavors. It's the ultimate comfort food, perfect for family dinners or special occasions.

Layered with savory meat sauce, creamy ricotta, and melty mozzarella, this lasagna will have everyone coming back for seconds.

Ingredients

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 24 oz marinara sauce
  • 15 oz ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Boil the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a pan, cook the ground beef with onion and garlic until browned. Drain fat.
  4. Add marinara sauce to the meat and simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, egg, Parmesan, salt, and pepper.
  6. In a baking dish, spread a layer of meat sauce, then noodles, then ricotta mixture, and mozzarella. Repeat layers.
  7. Top with remaining mozzarella and Parmesan.
  8. Bake for 30–40 minutes until cheese is bubbly and golden.
  9. Let it rest for 10 minutes before serving. Enjoy!
