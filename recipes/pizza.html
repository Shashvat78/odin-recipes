Cheesy Margherita Pizza

Description

A classic Margherita pizza with fresh basil, mozzarella, and tomato sauce.

Perfectly crispy crust with gooey cheese and bright, flavorful toppings.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Olive oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Spread sauce over the dough.
  3. Add cheese and basil.
  4. Drizzle with olive oil.
  5. Bake for 10–12 minutes until the crust is golden.
